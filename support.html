Frequently asked questions

How do I sign in?
Enter your phone number, then enter the one-time verification code (OTP) sent to you by SMS.

How do I earn cashback?
Open your personal QR code in the app and show it to the seller at checkout. The purchase and cashback are linked to your account automatically.

How do I delete my account?
Open the app, go to Profile → Delete account. Your personal data will be removed.
