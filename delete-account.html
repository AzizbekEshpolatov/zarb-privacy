ZARB — Delete Your Account and Data

This page explains how to delete your ZARB account and the personal data associated with it.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (recommended)

  1. Open the ZARB app and sign in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and personal data will be deleted.

Option 2 — Request by email

If you cannot access the app, send a deletion request to support@zarb.uz from the phone number / email linked to your account. We will process it within 30 days.

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, we delete your personal data, including:

  • Account data — phone number and verification codes;
  • Profile data — full name, profession, region, and profile photo;
  • Usage data — purchase history, cashback balance, and saved products;
  • Technical data — push notification token and authentication tokens.

What data may be kept

Certain records (for example, transaction/accounting records) may be retained for a limited period where required by law, after which they are deleted.
